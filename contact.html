Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What technologies and services do you specialize in?

We specialize in Odoo ERP solutions, C# desktop applications, Python development, web applications, infrastructure management, network design, cybersecurity solutions, cloud migration, and comprehensive remote IT services. Our team has extensive experience with modern frameworks, cloud technologies, and enterprise infrastructure.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple websites can take 2-4 weeks, while complex ERP implementations may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages for all our solutions. This includes bug fixes, updates, security patches, and feature enhancements to keep your systems running smoothly.

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in system integration and can work with your existing infrastructure. We ensure seamless integration while maintaining data integrity and minimizing downtime.

What's your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. Contact us for a customized quote.

Do you offer remote services?

Yes, we are a remote-first company and can serve clients worldwide. We use modern collaboration tools and maintain clear communication throughout the project lifecycle.

What infrastructure services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive infrastructure services including cloud migration, server administration, network design and management, cybersecurity solutions, backup and disaster recovery, data center operations, and 24/7 monitoring and support.

Do you provide 24/7 support for infrastructure?

Yes, we offer 24/7 monitoring and support for critical infrastructure systems. Our team provides proactive monitoring, rapid response to issues, and comprehensive maintenance to ensure maximum uptime and performance.
